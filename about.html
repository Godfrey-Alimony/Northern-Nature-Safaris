About Us

Northern Nature Safaris. is a specialized tour operator organizing safaris in Uganda. We’ve been doing this for over 10 years, so we really do know how to tailor-make the perfect safari for you. We work with our extensive network of contacts, both local and international to keep up-to date with the latest changes in the industry. We are also fully registered& Licensed by the Uganda Tourism Board to operate in the industry..

Our operations !:

We do not just “sell” safaris – we operate safari & run other tour activities as well. This means you are a Northern Nature Safaris guest, on a Northern Nature Safaris trip, where in majority of the cases our own staff are taking care of you. It’s our people on the grounds who make our company – we believe they do their best to serve you on your trip with us. . Our Safari vehicles are purposely built for the job in hand and are approved for use by the local authorities.
