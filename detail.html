Wecome to Murchision Falls

Checkout out our tripes.

Our Destination and the time are as follows.

  • 1- Boat (Murchision) drive.
  • 2- Game (Murchision)drive .
  • 3- Birds, (Murchision) watching.
  • 4- Camping (Murchision) with tent.
  • 5- Dinner (Murchision) Evening.

We always start our tripe in the morning with picking our packed breakfast,then we proceed to Game drive.

We come back for lunch at comping site at 1.00pm then we rest and at 3.00pm we go for boat drive to Murchision falls then we come back and followed with a Dinner.
